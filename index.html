About MoodFlix

MoodFlix is a mood-based movie recommendation app. You can either type how you're feeling or submit a message, and our system will analyze your mood and suggest a list of movies tailored to your emotional state. Save favorites, leave mood-based reviews, and find the film that fits your vibe.
